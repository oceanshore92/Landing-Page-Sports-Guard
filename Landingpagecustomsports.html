What is the Custom SportsGuard Program?

Our program provides high-quality, custom-fitted mouthguards to student athletes. Unlike boil-and-bite guards, our SportsGuards are created from a precise impression of each athlete's teeth, ensuring a perfect fit. This enhances protection against dental injuries, improves breathing, and allows for clear communication on the field or court.
